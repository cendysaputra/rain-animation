Every drop is a beginning.

Rain never asks for permission to fall. It simply falls — and from there, everything grows.

There is a calm behind the sound of rain repeating itself. Each drop touches the surface, forms a ripple, then fades — but its trace remains.

Like the process we go through: repetitive, slow, sometimes invisible. But behind it all, something is growing.

You don't need to understand every drop. Just be still, listen, and let it flow.
